# Mental Health Risk-R analysis
A data analysis project examining factors associated with mental health risk using R.

## Tools
- R
- R Markdown
- ggplot2
- dplyr

## Key Analysis
- Gender and mental health risk distribution
- Health and lifestyle factors vs mental health risk
- Mental health status vs mental health risk
- Work-Related factors vs mental health risk
